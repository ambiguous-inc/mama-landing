how it feels

Build your training library by swiping yes.

We pull in your existing content from Instagram, YouTube, and your camera roll. You swipe through lessons, approving the ones you want to teach. We handle the rest — structuring, scheduling, push notifications, everything.

  • 1
    Connect your accounts. One tap each — Instagram, YouTube, TikTok. We pull your last 90 days of content into a sortable feed.
  • 2
    Swipe through your own posts. Like a dating app, but for lessons. Yes goes into the curriculum. No is hidden. Swipe right on what you teach best.
  • 3
    We auto-build the syllabus. Your approved content gets sequenced into a 12-week mission plan, with daily missions and weekly check-ins.
  • 4
    Fido learns your voice. Our AI assistant trains on your captions and answers your clients' questions the way you would.
