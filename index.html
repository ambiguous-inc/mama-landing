What it is

A daily-mission training app that wears your name, not ours.

Mama gives trainers and creators a complete client-facing app — curriculum, AI assistant, payments, branding — running under their own subdomain. We disappear behind your brand. Your audience never sees ours.

What we provide

A twelve-week daily curriculum (84 lessons). A reference library of more than 450 articles. An AI assistant tuned to your voice. Push notifications, streaks, check-ins. Stripe Connect billing under your name. A branded subdomain on day one.

What you provide

Your method, your colors, your voice. A handful of intro videos. Your client list, if you have one. We’ll handle the rest. You can swap our default force-free curriculum for your own at any time — the platform bends to your method, not the other way around.

Who it’s for

Working professionals with audiences who already trust them.

i.
Certified force-free trainers CCPDT, KPA, IAABC, PPG members building a digital practice alongside in-person work.
ii.
Dog content creators (100K+) Educators with engaged audiences ready to convert into a daily-use product, not another course.
iii.
LIMA-aligned behaviorists Specialists in reactivity, anxiety, separation, or rescue work who want a structured client touchpoint.
iv.
Method-driven studios Small training businesses ready to package their methodology as a subscription product.
